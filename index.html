Common questions

Can't find your answer? Chat with us

Can I open an Ansvel account?

Yes. Anyone in our supported countries (Nigeria, Ghana, with more coming soon) can open a personal or business account. All you need is a phone number, a valid government-issued ID, and a selfie for biometric verification. Sign-up takes under 60 seconds.

Is my money secure on Ansvel?

Yes. All funds are protected with bank-grade AES-256 encryption, TLS 1.3, multi-factor authentication, and continuous fraud monitoring, in line with CBN and NDPR requirements.

Which countries are currently supported?

Ansvel currently operates in Nigeria and Ghana, with Kenya and additional West African markets launching soon.

How do merchants join the Ansvel marketplace?

Merchants can apply directly in the app or on the Business Portal. Verification typically takes 1-2 business days once documents are submitted.

Are there fees for using Ansvel?

Wallet-to-wallet transfers within Ansvel are free. Small fees apply to card top-ups, withdrawals, and cross-border transfers — all shown upfront before you confirm.

Does Ansvel have a developer API?

Yes. Our developer API covers payments, KYC/KYB, and payouts, with full documentation, a sandbox environment, and dedicated developer support.
